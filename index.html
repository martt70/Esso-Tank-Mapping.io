Retail Gasoline Station - Tank to Dispenser Hose Mapping Document

The Client has hired Agency Media to assist Sol Petroleum Bahamas in providing a detailed electronic document to identify the tank piping network of each underground storage tank in relation to each dispenser hose connection. The client is expected to utilize the information to improve its Inventory Reconciliation procedures in addition to improving their site specific marketing intiatives.

The following deliverables have been agreed upon and provided below:

  1. A Site Map for each station.
  2. A Daily Dispenser Meter Form for each station.
  3. A Daily Inventory Worksheet.
  4. A Summary Inventory Worksheet specific to each tank(s).
